Our Programs

2024

Natty Stays Club members get annual free stays as a number of NFT POAP tokens, varying by tier and issued on January 1st annually. These can be traded or transferred. Nattynites ensures at least 10 years, at most 30 years of these allocations, redeemable at partner properties in the NFT's linked country. Learn more
Nattynites Growth Rewards Nattynites will allocate a share of its net profits as dividend rewards quarterly to its club members. These dividends will be deposited directly into the wallets holding the NFTs. As Nattynites expands, the amount of these dividends will also rise.
Dealer Program Don't miss out, become a dealer today! This is your chance to sign up become one of our premier partners before anyone else! Take the lead in driving Nattynites NFT sales and gain automatic entry to our privileged whitelist. Unlock amazing NFT discounts and additional perks. It's an exciting opportunity – grab it now!
Referral Program Embrace the role of an ambassador for Nattynites and its partners, open to all club members and no need to sign up! Earn up to 5% rewards for each guest stay at partner properties. This is your opportunity to build a lifelong, passive income. The more you promote, the more you earn. Take charge and be your own boss! Learn more
Natty Quests Enjoy enhanced rewards such as discounts, upgrade, and exclusive perks for frequent use of our alliance partners' services, open to all club members and guests. Delight in special offers including weekend getaways, gourmet dining, VIP event tickets, and adventure experiences like scuba diving. These rewards aim to elevate your experience and reward your loyalty.

2025

Dapp The NFT-fi Dapp is an innovative solution for boosting the liquidity of your NFTs. It allows club members to stake and refinance their NFTs, thereby enhancing investment potential and unlocking funds bound to your digital assets. This Dapp transforms your NFTs into powerful tools for financial growth and accessibility.
Utility Token ICO The Utility token, set to launch in 2025, is exclusively designed for purchasing additional experiences, services, and travel packages offered by Nattynites and our alliance partners. Notably, only club members will have the privilege to participate in the pre-sale of these tokens, granting early access to a range of exclusive benefits and opportunities.
Global Nomad Program Subscribe to the Global Nomad Program and unlock a continuous holiday experience across our alliance partners' properties at a fixed price. This program offers an endless journey of exploration and luxury, seamlessly connecting you with exceptional stays around the world. And never tired of one place!
DAO Community Club members have the opportunity to join our exclusive DAO community, a gathering of talented people from various sectors. This community is a hub for professional and cultural exchange, playing a crucial role in shaping the future strategies and growth of Nattynites. It's a space where ideas flourish and members contribute to our collective success.
Global Redeem Ecosystem Nattynites leverages the versatility of redeemable NFTs for accessing and redeeming service vouchers, airdrops and products at attractive prices from our alliance partners. It offers a diverse array of applications, ranging from enjoying gourmet meals and relaxing massages to experiencing top-tier performances, indulging in travel packages, and luxury experiences and activities.

2026 and Vision

DAO-DRIVEN VILLA RESORT This is an exciting venture where we plan to build our own villa resort, offering DAO members an exclusive opportunity to become co-owners. This will be spearheaded by hospitality experts, while the strategic decisions will be collectively made by DAO. As a part of this program, DAO members not only get a say in the project's direction but also stand to gain lifelong passive income.
Property Tokenization The program offers a unique opportunity to invest in alliance partners' properties, either partially or fully, using our utility tokens. This investment entitles investors to a portion of the profits generated from the property. Additionally, the property can continue the convenience of using our platform for booking and redeeming features, seamlessly integrating investment and user experience.
