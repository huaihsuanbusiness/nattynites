Membership

Flexible Membership Control
1. Mint or Purchase Your Membership NFT
Acquire your tiered membership NFT through our platform or secondary marketplaces. Each tier offers a unique set of benefits, but these perks remain inactive until you subscribe.
2. Activate Your Membership
Subscribe to activate your perks. As long as your subscription is active, you'll enjoy discounts, VIP access, and upgrades. You can cancel the subscription at any time, and if the NFT is sold or transferred, the subscription will automatically stop. The longer you hold and stake your NFT, the more valuable your perks become.
3. Sell or Transfer with Ease
Your Membership NFT is a tradable asset. When you sell or transfer it, the subscription will automatically stop, allowing the new owner to activate it when ready.

2. Transfer or Sell on Secondary Markets
If your plans change, you can easily sell or transfer your booking on open marketplaces like OpenSea. This allows you to recoup value or pass the reservation to someone else.
3. Enjoy a Flexible Stay
Whether you keep or transfer the booking, experience your accommodation with peace of mind, knowing your options remain open until the last minute.
