Empowering Your Path to Unmatched Success

Natty Stays

Natty Stay is designed to infuse real-world value into your NFTs, making them more useful, more liquid, and more resilient, even in a bear market. Members who acquire the Nattynites membership NFT will receive the Natty Gems NFT, which can be used to redeem stays at hotels and enjoy benefits from our affiliate partners. The distribution of these rewards will last for a minimum of 15 years and up to 30 years, with the possibility of enjoying up to 60 nights of stay per year, depending on your membership tier.

Engage

To become a member, obtain the Nattynites Membership NFT.

Distribute

Biannually, Natty Gems NFTs will be allocated to your NFT/wallet according to your membership tier, providing up to 60 nights annually for at least 15 years.

Redeem

Redeem Natty Gems on Nattynites's Dapp (to be launched soon) to enjoy your stay at any of Nattynites's partner accommodations.

Outlook

Future Feature: After booking, your stay will be converted into a Stay NFT, which can be transferred or sold on the secondary market if your travel plans change. 

Natty Quests

Natty Quests Program is a groundbreaking fusion of luxurious hospitality and cutting-edge blockchain gamification. This innovative program offers guests of our partner hotels a captivating adventure into the realm of digital collectibles, represented by Equipment NFTs. Spanning attributes like Hotel and Activity, these NFTs aim to transform the hospitality experience, combining the pleasure of physical stays with the thrill of competition and digital asset accumulation.

Airdrop

Stay at any of our partner hotels for a chance to receive an Equipment NFT airdrop.

Attributes

Each Equipment NFT has one of five attributes initially: Hotel, Hedonist, Beauty, Gourmet, or Activity. Duplicates can be sold on the marketplace.

Fusion

Fuse a specific number of Equipment NFTs into badges, granting access to perks related to their properties.

Reward

Badges and stay count contribute to your leaderboard ranking. Half of the marketplace transaction tax forms a reward pool, with higher-ranked participants having a better chance to win rewards.
