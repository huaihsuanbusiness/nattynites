Highlight of This Week

Potique Hotel

Get the celebrity treatment with world-class service at Potique Hotel

Set in Nha Trang, 1.2 km from Nha Trang Beach, Potique Hotel offers accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a fitness centre and a terrace. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a kids' club and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, a business centre and currency exchange for guests.
Address: 22 Hung Vuong Street, Loc Tho Ward, Nha Trang City, Nha Trang, Vietnam
Website:  https://potiquehotel.com/
